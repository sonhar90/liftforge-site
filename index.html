Why LiftForge?

Heaviest actual wins

If you planned 80 kg but hit 90 kg on any successful set, next time starts from ≥ 90 kg — even for custom exercises.

StrongLifts progression

5×5 progression is built-in, but the “heaviest actual” rule overrides automatic bumps when you outperform the plan.

Custom exercises

Use any name/ID. We normalize IDs on create and persist working weights per exercise ID across sessions.

AMRAP friendly

Target reps of 0 mean AMRAP; any positive weight logs as a success for progression.

Local-first & private

All data stays on your device using iOS storage. No accounts, no trackers, no ads.

12-week sequencing

Simple A/B cadence expanded over 12 weeks, with a nice “Rest/Bonus” flow on off days.

FAQ

How are working weights chosen?

We store one working weight per exercise ID. A StrongLifts bump applies on success, but if you lifted heavier than planned and hit target reps (or AMRAP), we clamp the new working weight to your heaviest successful set.

Does it work with custom exercises?

Yes. Custom IDs are normalized when created. Working weights are keyed by the ID, not the display name.

What data do you collect?

None. The app is local-first and stores everything on your device. See the Privacy Policy.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet — currently iOS only.
