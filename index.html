Why LiftForge?

Heaviest actual wins

If you planned 80 kg but hit 90 kg on any successful set, your next session starts from ≥ 90 kg — even for custom exercises.

Standard 5×5 progression

Automatic 5×5 bumps on success. When you outperform the plan, the heaviest successful set overrides the bump.

Custom exercises

Use any exercise name. IDs are normalized on create, and working weights are stored per exercise ID.

AMRAP friendly

Target reps of 0 means AMRAP. Any positive weight counts as a success for progression.

Local-first & private

Everything stays on your device using iOS storage. No analytics. No tracking. No ads.

12-week sequencing

Simple A/B cadence expanded across 12 weeks, with a smooth “Rest/Bonus” flow on off days.

FAQ

How are working weights chosen?

We store one working weight per exercise ID. A Standard 5×5 bump applies on success, but if you lifted heavier than planned and met target reps (or AMRAP), we clamp the new working weight to your heaviest successful set.

Does it work with custom exercises?

Yes. Custom IDs are normalized when created. Working weights are keyed by the ID, not the display name.

What data do you collect?

None. LiftForge is local-first and stores everything on your device. See the Privacy Policy.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet — currently iOS only.
