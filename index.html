FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need an account?

No. LiftForge works without any account. Open the app and start lifting. Your data stays on your device.

Does it work offline?

Yes, completely. Plans, logging, history and progress all work without an internet connection.

How does auto-progression work?

After each successful session, LiftForge automatically increases your working weights for the next one. If you miss reps, it adjusts — keeping you progressing without overloading.

Can I build my own program?

Yes. The Program Builder lets you create a custom weekly schedule, add any exercises, and set your own sets, reps and weights. You can also start a blank session and add exercises on the fly.

How does the rest timer work?

The timer runs in the background and sends a push notification when your rest is done — so you can lock your phone and get notified when it's time to lift again.

What's included in Pro?

Pro adds unlimited saved sessions, full PR tracking with details, CSV export of your training history, and advanced progress charts. Everything else — all programs, progression, history, and the rest timer — is free forever.

What if I switch phones?

Export your history as CSV (Pro) before switching. iCloud backup may also restore app data if you restore from a full backup.
