Why LiftForge?

Automatic 5×5 bumps after a successful session. If you outperform the plan, your heaviest successful set overrides the bump.

How it works

1. Start a session

Open the plan and lift. We show your current working weight per exercise.

2. Log sets, your way

Record reps and weight. AMRAP? Set target reps to 0 and just lift.

3. We update your base

If you meet target reps on any set (or AMRAP with weight > 0), we clamp the next working weight to your heaviest successful set.

FAQ

How are working weights chosen?

We store one working weight per exercise ID. A Standard 5×5 bump applies on success, but if you lifted heavier than planned and hit target reps (or AMRAP with weight > 0), we clamp the new working weight to your heaviest successful set.

Does it work with custom exercises?

Yes. Custom IDs are normalized when created. Working weights are keyed by the ID, not the display name.

What data do you collect?

None. The app is local-first and stores everything on your device. See the Privacy Policy.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet — currently iOS only.
