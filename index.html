Progress made easy.

LiftForge is a private iOS strength log that makes progression automatic and obvious. One working weight per exercise, updated by plan or by your performance — whichever is higher.

Get on TestFlight No account · No tracking · On-device

Why LiftForge?

Heaviest actual wins

If you planned 80 kg but hit 90 kg on any successful set, your next session starts from ≥ 90 kg. Works for any exercise ID — including your custom ones.

Standard 5×5 progression

Built-in 5×5-style increases when targets are met. If you lift heavier than planned, the heaviest-actual update overrides the automatic bump.

Custom exercises

Create any exercise name. IDs are normalized once, and working weights are stored per ID across sessions.

AMRAP-friendly

Target reps of 0 mean AMRAP; any positive weight counts as a success for progression.

12-week sequencing

A clear A/B cadence expanded across 12 weeks, with an optional Rest/Bonus flow.

Local-first & private

Everything is stored on your device using iOS storage. No analytics. No tracking. No ads.

How it works

1) Pick a plan

Start with Standard 5×5 or build your own 7-day week that repeats over 12 weeks.

2) Run a guided session

Adjust weight, log reps, use the built-in rest timer, and add notes. AMRAP sets use reps = 0.

3) Save — we update

After saving, each exercise’s working weight becomes the higher of (a) the plan’s 5×5 bump or (b) your heaviest successful set. Zero-weight sets are ignored.
