Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-08-30

LiftForge is a private, local-first iOS training log.

Data we store

  • On your device only: workout plans, sets, reps, weights, notes, and preferences.
  • No analytics or tracking: we do not collect usage data, crash reports, or personal information.
  • Optional contact: if you email us, we’ll see your address and message. We use it only to reply.

Third-parties

No third-party SDKs or trackers are used in the app.

Your rights

Your workout data is on your device. Delete the app to remove it. For email requests, contact privacy@YOUR-DOMAIN.

Updates

We may update this policy as features evolve. Changes will be posted here.
